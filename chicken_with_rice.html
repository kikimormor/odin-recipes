Chicken with rice

Home

Description

This recipe might take some time, but it is still easy peasy! You can give yourself a mani/pedi while waiting for it to cook.

Ingredients

  • chicken - 500g
  • mushrooms - 100g
  • rice - 1 coffee cup
  • olive oil - 1 teacup
  • carrot
  • black pepper
  • savory

Steps

  1. Rinse the chicken, cut it into pieces. Cut the carrot into pieces and place them in the pan. Add the black pepper and savory on the chicken.
  2. Add the olive oil and 1 coffee cup of water. Put it in the oven for 30 minutes at 250C heat.
  3. While you wait, clean the mushrooms and cut them into pieces. Clean the rice until the water is clear.Then let it soak for 15 minutes.
  4. When the timer runs out, take the pan out and flip the chicken pieces upside down. Place the rice and the mushrooms below the chicken, add 2 coffeecups of water just enough so the rice is covered.
  5. Place it in the oven, and when the water starts boiling, put the timer to however many minutes it says on the package of the rice.
