Fried Toast

Home

Description

Not only is this a simple recipe but also time-efficient! Enjoy some protein with some yummy carbs!

Ingredients

  • two eggs
  • 4 slices of rice flour bread
  • unrefined sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl and start scrambling until there are bubbles.
  2. Smear 1 drop of the oil on the pan and put it at medium heat to heat up.
  3. After a minute, dip the bread into the egg bowl fully and scrape out excess egg off the bread with a stencil
  4. Put it on the pan and wait a minute, then flip it on the other side
  5. Do the same for all the slices
